About the Platform

Here will have a brief statement on the overall message of Advocacy and Action
Poilicies will be listed below

Cost & Savings

- Advocate for lower cost by reducing fees/increasing value

- Fixing broken tuition payment system

- Advocate for lower tuition for online learning

- Free Textbook program

- Expanding current OER Program

- Use research from IllinoisPIRG to strengthen our cause

- $15/hr on-campus minimum wage

- More grants and scholarships

- Free I-clicker program

- Usually used once for one class, doesn't make sense that students need to keep buying these

Counselors & Mental Health

- More counselors on campus

- Crisis counselors

- Continuing current USG Administration’s work on this

- More mental health workshops

- Mental health Hotline - UIC Safe App

COVID-19 & Online Learning

- Ensure the shift back to in-person learning is safe.

- Free masks and sanitizer across campus.

- Sanitizing sprays for populated areas

- Wall Mounts

- Air Disinfectant

- Open campus up for social distancing.

Organizational Relationships

- Ensure USG has a great relationship with organizations on campus.

- Have a collaborative effort with more organizations on campus.

- Fix the funding structure and make it more accessible to those who need it.

- Market USG’s presence on campus.

- Work with CSI to urge clubs to create a relationship with USG
